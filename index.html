About Me

I’m Michael Gvozdek

I turn the world around me into vibrant, creative projects.
From LEGO to crayons, I’ve been creating my whole life. I’m studying Global Business and Digital Art
at the University of Waterloo’s Stratford School of Interaction Design and Business. Digital design has been my focus
since highschool and I’ve picked up experience with Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects, and Premier Pro.
In combination with my design skills, I have experience with coding creative projects in Python, Java, Javascript, and HTML/CSS.
My passion for BMX has built the foundation of my creative style, with the vibrant, bold, and gritty nature of skateparks bleeding into my designs. Time leading my highschool yearbook team as well as directing my first documentary
has given me excellent team management and collaboration skills.
